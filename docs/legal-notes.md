Use v3dfy only with material you own or are authorized to process. Do not use
the application to bypass DRM or other access controls.

Before distributing an installer, review the exact licenses and redistribution
requirements for:

- FFmpeg and the selected FFmpeg build configuration.
- Embedded Python.
- PyTorch and other Python packages included by the engine.
- nunif/iw3 or any compatible replacement engine.
- Every pretrained model.

Include required license texts, notices, attribution, and model provenance in
`licenses/`. Placeholder files do not grant redistribution rights.
